PseudoRandom Adventures

Welcome to the Adventures. In this game mode, you will get to create a character from the options you see below and play out some PseudoRandom adventures.
Once you have browsed the options below and picked something you feel confortable with, you will start a new Campaign for that character. Your journey begins as a young adventurer arriving in the Big City.
The campaign will allow you to take on a set of quests in the form of Combat Encounters or Scripted Interactions. Combat Encounters will earn your party experience, gold and renown, while Scripted Encounters will bring setbacks and opportunities for your characters to deal with.
